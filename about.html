About

Alma Hoang

Self taught french macaron baker based in Fort Worth, Texas

"I believe they are unique in the sense that every flavor I try to make is different from the regular flavors you see at a French bakery"

I worked hard for six months trying to come up with the perfect macaron recipe. At the end of 2015 I was still struggling to make my recipe work but early 2016 was my first successful attempt at making the perfect not-so-sweet macaron.
