FAQs

Are macarons gluten free?

Yes. All macarons are made with almond flour, confectioner's sugar and egg whites - making them naturally gluten free.

Are any of the macarons dairy free?

Yes. Our Margarita macaron is dairy free, but we always try to incorporate dairy-free macarons monthly.

How much are the macarons?

The macarons are $2.20 each.

General care instructions

To maintain freshness, keep your macarons refrigerated for up to 3 days after purchase.

What is the order process for customized orders?

I usually take orders up to two weeks in advance. Let me know the date and time of the event.
The macarons are delivered a day in advance.

What form of payment do you accept?

I accept cash or card.

Privacy policy
